G. E. Wilson was an American politician who served as a member of the Oklahoma Senate between 1914 and 1918. He was the only Socialist Party member to win election to the Oklahoma Senate. ==Oklahoma Senate== Wilson ran for the Oklahoma Senate in 1914. He won the general election running as a candidate for the Socialist Party.
