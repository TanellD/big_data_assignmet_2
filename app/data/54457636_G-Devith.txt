Ngoun Chan Devith (; born 5 October 1994) better known by his stage name G-Devith () or Dit-Way (), is a Cambodian rapper, singer-songwriter, and music producer. He used to record for Rasmey Hang Meas Production but in 2019, he created his own label under the name of Dit-Way Nation.
