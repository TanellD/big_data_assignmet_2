G. Nageswara Reddy () is an Indian film director, known for his works in Telugu cinema. He worked as an assistant director to S. V. Krishna Reddy, for several films like Rajendrudu Gajendrudu, Mayalodu, Yamaleela, Subhalagnam, Number One, Ghatotkachudu, Vajram and Maavichiguru. ==Filmography== Year Film 2001 6 Teens 2001 Ide Naa Modati Premalekha 2002 Girl Friend 2003 Oka Radha Iddaru Krishnula Pelli 2003 Nenu..
