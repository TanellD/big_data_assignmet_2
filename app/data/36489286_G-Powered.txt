G-Powered is a Finnish Christian Eurodance band. The band was formed in 2005 by Kimmo Korpela and Miia Rautkoski. They started touring in spring 2006 and their first album, Odottanut oon, was released in November, during the Maata Näkyvissä Festival.G-Powered In 2009, G-Powered reached third place on the Eurodanceweb Award with their song "Kohti unelmaa".Eurodanceweb Rautkoski left G-Powered in early 2013, replaced by new vocalist Susanna Korhonen. ==Discography== * Odottanut oon (I've Been Waiting) (2006)Fono.fi - Äänitetietokanta (in Finnish) * Todellisuus (The Reality) (2008)Fono.fi - Äänitetietokanta (in Finnish) * Trust (2010)G-Powered - Trust (CD, Album) at Discogs * Tahdon luottaa (I Want to Trust) (2011) ==References== Category:Finnish Eurodance groups Category:Finnish Christian musical groups
