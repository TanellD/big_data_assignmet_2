Gwendolyn Veronica Green (born June 9, 1959) is an American politician. She is a member of the Pennsylvania House of Representatives representing the 190th District since 2023 and previously in 2020. ==Early life and education== Green was born on June 9, 1959. She graduated from Murrell Dobbins Vocational High School in 1977 and later attended George Meany Labor College. ==Political career== In February 2020, Green won a special election to fill the 190th District seat of Pennsylvania State Representative Movita Johnson-Harrell, who had pled guilty to charity theft in 2019. Green failed to win the 2020 general primary election, and was defeated by fellow Democrat Amen Brown. In 2022, Brown was redistricted to the 10th District.
