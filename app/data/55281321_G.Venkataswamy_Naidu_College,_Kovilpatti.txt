G.Venkataswamy Naidu College(Autonomous), Kovilpatti (G.V.N. College) is a general degree college in Kovilpatti, Thoothukudi district, Tamil Nadu. It was established in 1966. The college is affiliated with Manonmaniam Sundaranar University.
