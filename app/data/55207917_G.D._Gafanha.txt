Grupo Desportivo da Gafanha, founded in 1957, is a sports club from Gafanha da Nazaré, Ílhavo, Portugal.Official site of G.D. Gafanha (http://www.gdg.pt/) The club is mainly known by its football team which competes in the third tier of Portuguese league system since 2014–15 season and plays its home games in Complexo Desportivo da Gafanha. Their kit manufacturer is Macron.
