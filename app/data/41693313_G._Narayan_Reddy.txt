G. Narayan Reddy (c. 1926 - 16 December 1998) was an Indian politician and Member of Parliament of India. He was a member of the 3rd Lok Sabha and represented the Adilabad constituency of Telangana. Reddy was a member of the Congress political party. He was murdered on 16 December 1998 at the age of 72. ==Political career== Reddy was the third elected M.P from Adilabad constituency.
