Gobinda Chandra High School () was established in 1969 in the district of Jajpur in Odisha, India. ==Campus and building== The school is situated in aunri panchayat near the villages such as Dihakula,aunri, kuanrpur, gobindpur . It has three large halls, a two storage hudco building and a separate building for office, teacher's common room and headmaster's chamber. ==References== Category:Schools in Odisha Category:1969 establishments in Orissa Category:Educational institutions established in 1969
