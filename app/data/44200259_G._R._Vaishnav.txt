G R Vaishnav (born 11 February 1990) is a member of the India men's national volleyball team. He wears the Number 1 or 11 Jersey for the Team. He also plays for Indian overseas bank volleyball team. He hails from Coimbatore presently at Chennai. He played the 2009 Junior world championship at Pune, India which secured fourth and it is the very first time for India to reach fourth position in U-21 World championship.
