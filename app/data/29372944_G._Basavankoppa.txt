G. Basavankoppa is a village in Dharwad district of Karnataka, India.Village Directory , 2001 Census of India == Demographics == As of the 2011 Census of India there were 292 households in G. Basavankoppa and a total population of 1,660 consisting of 853 males and 807 females. There were 234 children ages 0-6. ==References== Category:Villages in Dharwad district
