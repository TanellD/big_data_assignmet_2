George Stanley Wilson (1879-1958) was an American architect and builder based in Riverside, California. Wilson was born in Bournemouth, England in 1879. He came to Riverside, California in 1895 at age 16, with his family. He was buried in Evergreen Cemetery, Riverside's first cemetery. A number of his works are listed on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places for their architecture.
