Pulla Reddy Sweets is a pure ghee Indian sweets and snacks manufacturer based in Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh. It has a chain of retail sweet shop outlets in Hyderabad and Kurnool, which were started by G. Pulla Reddy. ==History== Pullareddy began selling sweets (Burfi) in 1948, when he was aged 28, using a small cart in Denkanikottai, a small town in Tamil Nadu.
