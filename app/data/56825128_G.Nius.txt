G.Nius is a 1988 French action video game developed by Lankhor for Amiga and Atari ST. == Plot == The player is a robot named G.Nius who has crash-landed his spaceship. He needs to escape the ship before it vaporizes by visiting every room to turn off a switch. == Development == The game experienced significant distribution problems. The game was prevented from being sold in parts of France. == Reception == The Games Machine felt the game could be annoying and confusing at times.
