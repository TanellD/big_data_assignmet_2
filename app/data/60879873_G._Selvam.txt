Ganesan Selvam () is an Indian politician. He was elected to the Lok Sabha, lower house of the Parliament of India from Kancheepuram, Tamil Nadu in the 2019 Indian general election as member of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam. == Family and Education == He completed his Master of Commerce in Pachaiyappa's College for Men, Kanchipuram in 2000. He received M.Phil in Alagappa University in 2013. He and his wife S.
