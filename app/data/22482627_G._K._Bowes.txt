Gina Keali'inohomoku Bowes (born December 12)Birthday: * * is an American voice actress known for playing Senna in the anime movie Bleach: Memories of Nobody. She had other roles, including Kiyal Bachika in Gurren Lagann, Little Red Riding Hood and Little Bo Peep in Happily N'Ever After 2: Snow White Another Bite @ the Apple, Asuka Kazama in Street Fighter X Tekken, and Daria, Freida and Fran in Alpha and Omega 4: The Legend of the Saw Tooth Cave.
