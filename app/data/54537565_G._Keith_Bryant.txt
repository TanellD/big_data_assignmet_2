G. Keith Bryant (born 1959) is an American firefighter who was head of the US Fire Administration from 2017 to 2021. ==Career== Bryant was a firefighter starting in 1977 before becoming the fire chief of Oklahoma City from 2005 to 2017. He was also the president of the International Association of Fire Chiefs.
